Sponsors

The Third International Conference on Computing, Communication and Learning (CoCoLe)-2024 will take place in NIT, Warangal during September 13th - September 15th, 2024.. The CoCoLe conference series has become a platform for computer science researchers from India and all over the world to exchange research results and ideas on the foundations and applications of computing, communication and learning technologies. Like the last edition, the proceedings will be published by Springer in the series of CCIS.
Sponsoring the CoCoLe 2024 International Conference is an opportunity to become involved and get your name in front of attendees. If you are interested in becoming a sponsor, please review the information below.
